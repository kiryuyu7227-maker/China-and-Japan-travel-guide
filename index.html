One-click Access

Subway & Bus

Forget buying tickets. With Alipay, your phone is your pass for public transport in almost every Chinese city.

Step 1: Select Transport

On the Homepage, click the "Transport" icon.

One Code for All

This section holds the QR codes for Metro (Subway) and Bus.

Step 2: Check Your City

Alipay usually auto-detects your location. If not, switch it manually.

Note: I used "Guangzhou" in screenshots as an example. If you are in Shanghai, simply select Shanghai. The steps are identical.

Step 3: One-time Setup

If it's your first time in a city, you need to activate the card.

Step 4: Scan & Go

This is your digital ticket. Use it to enter and exit the station.

How to use:

  • Hold your phone screen about 5cm above the scanner at the gate.
  • The gate will open instantly.
  • Payment: It will calculate the distance and deduct money automatically when you exit.
Fast Access Next time you open "Transport", this QR code will appear immediately. No need to set up again!
JAPAN GUIDE

Subway & bus (Suica)

One card to rule them all. Trains, buses, and convenience stores.

Green vs. Red: Which one for you?

Japan offers two main types of digital Suica. I recommend the Green Suica for long-term use.

Feature Green Suica (Standard) Red Suica (Welcome)
Target Audience Residents / Long-term travelers Short-term Tourists
Validity 10 Years (from last use) 28 Days (Physical) / 180 Days (App)
Refundable? Yes (Balance is refundable) No (Use it or lose it)
Platform iPhone Only* iPhone Only*
Android User?
Unfortunately, most Android phones sold outside Japan do not have the required "FeliCa" chip. Please purchase a physical Suica card (Green or Red) at the airport or major stations.

Step 1: Open Wallet

No need to download a separate app. Use the native Apple Wallet.

Native Integration

Open the "Wallet" app on your iPhone and tap the (+) button at the top right corner.
